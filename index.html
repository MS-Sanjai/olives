FROM FORK TO FEAST
Unveiling the story of Our Hospitality Group.

Food Castle Group has been in the restaurant business for over a decade and was estab-lished based on its ground rules in customer satisfaction. The first outlet of Food Castle restaurant was launched during the year 2002, in Al Karama. Given our quality in service, we were provid-ed with the opportunity to expand our busi-ness to the Dubai International Airports wherein we were able to serve the airport staff and also cater to an international com-munity. We are highly honored to have com-menced business activities with Dubai Interna-tional Airport as it is known to be the 2nd bus-iest airport in the world by the international passenger traffic.
